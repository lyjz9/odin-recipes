Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a dessert that is filled with cheese, eggs, and sugar over a crispy crust on the side and sometimes topped with fruits. There are different types of cheescakes, including New York-style cheesecake and Vegan cheesecake. Since cheesecakes are made with natrual ingredients, there is a rich source of vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 7 tablespoons butter melted
  • 32 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2/3 cups sour cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 F. Combine graham cracker crust with sugar and add melted butter, sittring as needed. Add the crumbs inside 9" springform pan.
  2. Use a hand mixer to stir cream cheese until it is smooth. Add sugar and stir again.
  3. Add sour cream, vanilla extract, salt, and stir until well-combined.
  4. Add all of eggs in the mix and use spatula to scrape side of and bottom of bowl to make sure the ingredients are combined.
  5. Pour cheesecake batter into prepared springform pan and place pan on a cookie dheet that is lined with foil.
  6. Move the pan into the oven and bake on 325F for about 75 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and allow to cool on top of oven for about 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, use knife to loosen the crust from inside of the pan.
  8. Allow cheesecake to cool for another 1-2 hours until near room temperautre before puting it in the refreigeator and allow to cool for at least 6 hours.
