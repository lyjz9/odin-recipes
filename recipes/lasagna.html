Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish that is made with strips of pasta and layers of meat and vegetables. Tomato sauce and cheese are also added to add flavoring. Lasagna is popular because it is easy to assemble and there are many ways to make it. However, even though it is popular, lasagna is hard to make and some chef struggle to find the perfect lasgna. Specifically, burny edges and undercooked could prevent the extravagant taste that lasagna could have.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 2 crushed garlic
  • 2 tablesppon sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spices: 4 tablespoon of fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, and 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • Cheese: 16 oz ricotta cheese, 3/4 pound mozzarella, and 3/4 cup grated parmesan
  • Tomato products: 28 oz crushed tomatoes, 2 can of tomato sauce and tomato paste

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in oven until it is brown.
  2. Stir the tomato product with water. Season it with sugar, 2 tablespoon parsley, basil, 1 tablespoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, ad peper. Boil gently for 90 minutes while stirring.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling salted water. After cooked, rinse noodles with cold water
  4. Combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsely, 1/2 teaspoon salt in a mixing bowl and preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  5. Spread 1 1.2 cups of meat sauce in bottom of 9*13-inch baking bowl. Put 6 noodles on teaspoon of the meat sauce and then spread the ricotta cheese. Top it with 1/3 mozzarella cheese. Spoon 1 1/2 meat sacue over the mozzarella and sprinkle 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  6. Repeat layer with the top placed with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover the bowl with foil.
  7. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake another 25 minutes.
  8. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
